Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for developers in San Francisco. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated global conferences for tech enthusiasts, engineers, and innovators.

Our Mission

TechCon is driven by a mission to empower the next generation of developers and promote diversity and innovation in technology. Our goal is to create an inclusive space for learning, networking, and launching breakthrough ideas.

Notable Past Speakers

Elena Rivera

Elena is a pioneering AI researcher and the CTO of MindCore Labs. At TechCon 2021, she delivered a keynote on ethical machine learning.

Dr. Kamal Ahmed

Dr. Ahmed is a cybersecurity expert who spoke at TechCon 2022 about securing the internet of things (IoT) for the next decade.

Sasha Lin

Sasha, founder of Code4All, is an advocate for women in tech. Her session on equitable tech leadership was a highlight of TechCon 2023.
